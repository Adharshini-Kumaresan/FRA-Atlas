# Tribal Welfare Scheme Management System

A comprehensive web application designed for government staff and gram sabha members to analyze tribal population data and receive personalized scheme recommendations based on government DSS (Decision Support System) rules.

## Features

### 1. Tribal Data Dashboard
- **Key Metrics Overview**: Total ST population, hostels, students, and average ST percentage across Telangana
- **Interactive Charts**: Visual representation of ST population distribution and hostel allocation
- **District-wise Data Table**: Comprehensive demographic data with search and filter capabilities
- **Village Analysis**: Detailed village-level data with remote sensing metrics

### 2. Scheme Suggester
- **Intelligent Form**: Comprehensive data input form for village/tribal characteristics
- **DSS-based Recommendations**: Automated scheme suggestions based on government decision rules
- **Priority-based Results**: High, medium, and low priority scheme recommendations
- **Detailed Information**: Complete scheme details including eligibility, benefits, and implementation rules

## Supported Government Schemes

1. **Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Yojana (PMVDY)** - Forest produce value addition
2. **Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS)** - Quality education for ST children
3. **National Fellowship & Scholarship for ST Students** - Higher education support
4. **Pre-Matric & Post-Matric ST Scholarships** - Educational financial assistance
5. **Stand-Up India Scheme** - Entrepreneurship support
6. **PM JANMAN** - Support for Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups
7. **Forest Rights Act (FRA) Benefits** - Land rights and forest access
8. **Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP)** - Special budget allocation
9. **Jal Jeevan Mission** - Water infrastructure
10. **MGNREGA** - Employment guarantee
11. **PM-KISAN** - Direct income support for farmers
12. **National Health Mission** - Tribal health services
13. **ST Hostels** - Residential facilities
14. **DAJKUA/DAJGUA Convergence** - Integrated development support

## Decision Support System Rules

The application implements sophisticated DSS rules based on:

- **Population Demographics**: ST percentage, literacy rates, sex ratio
- **Infrastructure**: Water access, health facilities, education proximity
- **Economic Indicators**: Family income, unemployment, livelihood scores
- **Geographic Factors**: Forest cover, remote sensing data
- **Policy Compliance**: FRA status, PVTG classification, MFP dependency
